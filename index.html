About the Workshop

AeroDrive is an intensive two-days workshop where theory meets hands-on application. Unlike standard demonstrations, this program is meticulously designed so that each participant will individually assemble, fabricate, and pilot a drone from scratch.

We dive deep into the realities of drone technology, directly addressing the common problems, technical glitches, and real-world troubleshooting issues that arise during both the fabrication and flight phases. Beyond the hardware, we ensure that you will be fully prepared for the skies by providing comprehensive guidance on official drone licensing and regulatory compliance.
